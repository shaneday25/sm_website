Dance list 2025-2026


Town Morris – Bidford

Hanky dance – 135642 Line up. Forming set at the end. Single-step.


We Won’t Go Home ‘til Morning/ Cuckoo’s Nest – Bidford

Won’t Go Home ‘til Morning - Hanky dance – 1st chorus- circle partner flicking (odds first), 2nd chorus- circle partner punching (odds first). Followed by rounds and then – Cuckoo’s Nest - Hanky dance – 3 right-handed handshakes, 3 left-handed handshakes (tapping toe each time) then half-hey (Repeat). Single-step. Jumps.


Bluebells of Scotland – Adderbury

Stick dance – Odds strike twice, evens strike twice, odds strike twice, evens strike twice, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 + 6. (Repeat). Double step.


Vandals of Hammerwich – Lichfield

Stick dance – Right, Left, Partner, Partner, Partner, Side-step Right, Side-step Left, 3 Capers and Show. (Repeat finishing with a Clash). Double step.


In Wooden Shoon – Bidford

Hanky dance – 1st & 2nd chorus- Side-step right, side-step left, dance in circle, Bidford show-step x 2. 3rd & 4th chorus- Side-step right, side-step left, dance in circle, Bidford capers x 2. Single-step. Jumps.


Sweet Jenny Jones – Adderbury

Stick dance – Odds strike once, evens strike twice, odds strike once, evens strike twice, 1, 2, 3, 4. (Repeat). Heel, toe, heel-step.


Charlecote – Bidford

Stick dance – Together, right, left, together, together, left, right, together. Circular hey. (Repeat). Single-step. Jumps.


Sheriff’s Ride – Lichfield

Hanky dance – Heading-up and Heading-down have side-step flicks before the hockle-backs. Chorus: 2 flicks out and then step-back capers: Right-foot first and then Left-foot. (Repeat). Double-step.


Shepherd’s Hey – Bidford

Clapping – Clap, right knee, clap. Clap, left knee, clap. Clap, under, clap, under, clap in front, clap behind, clap in front. (Repeat). Or Short Stick – 3 strikes to beat, 3 strikes to beat. 3 single steps ending right foot in air. (Repeat). Single-step. Jumps.


Postman’s Knock – Adderbury

Stick dance – Odds strike twice, evens strike twice, odds strike twice, evens strike twice, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7. Odds strike twice, evens strike twice, odds strike twice, evens strike twice, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 + 7. 3 on floor. Double-step.


Old Trunko - Bidford

Hanky dance – Dance across set, turn to face partner on 5th beat, after 8th beat dance for further 4 single steps, jump and (starting on left leg) dance 3 single steps, kicking partner’s right leg on the 4th. Followed by: 1st chorus- side steps, 2nd chorus plain capers, 3rd chorus Bidford capers. Single-step. Jumps.


Ring of Bells - Lichfield

2 Stick dance – Right, Partner, Left, Partner, Right, Partner, Left, Partner, 4 capers, strike to the right at waist level 3 times. (Repeat). Heading-up & heading-out should form this line at the end: 5 6, 1 2, 3 4, 7 8. 3 capers and clash. Single-step.


Princess Royal – Bidford

Hanky dance – Side-step facing partner x 7, hop, side-step facing partner x 7, hop. (Repeat). Travel right with side- step x 7, hop, travel left with side-step x 7. Corners Bidford caper x 4 across set. Single-step to finish. Single-step. Jumps.


Constant Billy/ Morris Off – Bidford (All in)

Constant Billy - Hanky dance – Clap together, right, together, left, together, behind, together, in-front. Cross-over. (Repeat). Single-step. Jumps. Morris Off - Hanky dance – Bidford show-step x 2, single-step forwards then in circle. (Repeat). Single-step. Jumps.
