Come and join us

We are always pleased to welcome new members (both men and women) who are interested in Morris dancing and playing music.

If you have never tried Morris before, or you are an experienced dancer or musician, we would love to meet you.

Events

During the Summer months we danceout in local towns and villages usually with other local morris teams. We also make regular appearances on the Bancroft Gardens in Stratford where we enjoy introducing visitors to English Traditions.
